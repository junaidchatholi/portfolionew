Project Overview

The MESCE UAE Alumni app bridges the gap between alumni of MES College and resources in the UAE, offering career opportunities, blood donation networks, and a platform to post job vacancies.

Tools Used

Project Goals

    Job Search & Recruitment

    Enable alumni to easily search and apply for job opportunities

    Allow alumni to post job vacancies from their companies

    Create an efficient matching system between job seekers and recruiters

    Streamline the job application process within the alumni network

    Blood Donor Network

    Create a searchable directory of alumni blood donors

    Implement quick search filters for blood groups and location

    Enable immediate contact with potential donors

    Develop an emergency alert system for urgent blood requirements

    Alumni Connection

    Build a user-friendly profile system for alumni

    Facilitate direct communication between alumni

    Create a verified network of MES College graduates

    Enable location-based alumni search

Measurable Objectives:

    Achieve 70% alumni registration in the first year

    Generate 100+ job postings within first 6 months

    Create a database of at least 500 registered blood donors

    Maintain an average response time of under 30 minutes for blood requests

    Achieve 80% user satisfaction rate based on feedback

LEAN UX Methodology

Problem Statement

Alumni from MES College face challenges in connecting with peers in the UAE for career growth, networking, and emergency blood donation needs. Existing platforms are either too generic or lack targeted features for this community.

Job Search Hypothesis

    Geographic Relevance:

    Jobs in the UAE may not be highlighted on global platforms like LinkedIn or Indeed, making them harder to find.

    Industry Focus:

    Alumni often search for roles related to their fields of study or experience but face cluttered, irrelevant job listings.

    Ease of Use:

    Existing job platforms are complex for quick searches, leading to frustration and decreased usage.

Assumptions

    Alumni are more likely to apply for jobs if the listings are specific to their location (UAE) and industry.

    Adding search filters such as location, industry, and experience level will reduce the time spent finding relevant jobs.

    A clean, focused UI will increase engagement by making the process intuitive.

MVP Features for Job Search

The MVP was designed to include core functionalities, keeping the interface simple and targeted

    Search Bar: Allows users to search by job title or company name.

Filters:

    Location: Jobs in specific UAE regions (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, etc.)

    Industry: Categorized roles like IT, healthcare, education, etc.

    Experience Level: Entry-level, mid-level, senior positions.

Job Details Page:

    Job description, company details, and application link.

    “Apply Now” button for instant redirection to the employer's website or email.

Wireframes

User Journey for Job Search

    Discover: User logs into the app and selects the "Jobs" tab.

    Search: Enters keywords like "Software Engineer" and applies filters (e.g., location: Dubai, industry: IT)

Initiate Contact:

    Calls the recruiter directly to discuss the job opportunity

    Sends a WhatsApp message for further information.

    Connects via LinkedIn for a professional introduction.

Testing the Hypothesis

To validate the hypothesis, we ran a usability test with a prototype:

Participants:

10 alumni actively looking for jobs in the UAE.

Tasks Given:

    Find a software engineering job in Dubai.

    Apply filters for "Entry-level" positions.

    Search for notifications on similar jobs.

Key Metrics

    Task completion time: Less than 2 minutes.

    Number of relevant job results: Increased by 80% with filters applied

    User satisfaction score: 4.7/5 for search experience.

Continuous Iteration (Optimize)

Lean UX prioritizes feedback loops. Post-MVP testing led to further refinements:

    Visual Design Updates:

Focused on minimalism and accessibility to ensure ease of use for all alumni.

Results from MVP Testing

    Average Time to Find a Job: Reduced to 2.8 minutes compared to over 5 minutes on competitor platforms.

    Job Application Success Rate: Increased by 60% due to tailored filters and job notifications.

User Feedback:

    “The filters are so specific that I only see jobs that match my experience level.”
